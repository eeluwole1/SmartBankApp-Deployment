BankAccount Class

Description:

This file contains the implementation of the BankAccount class, which represents a bank account with functionalities to deposit, withdraw, and calculate service charges. The class implements the Observer pattern to notify observers of specific events.

calculate_service_charges(account: BankAccount) → float¶

Calculates the service charges based on the account balance and minimum balance.

If the account balance is greater than or equal to the minimum balance:

  • The service charge will be the base service charge.

If the account balance is less than the minimum balance:

  • The service charge will be the base service charge multiplied by the service charge premium.

Args:

account (BankAccount): The bank account for which the service charge is being calculated.

Returns:

float: The calculated service charge.

calculate_service_charges(account) → float¶

Calculates the service charges based on the account balance and overdraft limit.

If the account balance is greater than or equal to the overdraft limit:

  • The base service charge is applied.

Otherwise:

  • An overdraft penalty is added, calculated as the overdraft rate multiplied by the overdraft amount.

Args:

account (BankAccount): The bank account for which the service charge will be calculated.

Returns:

float: The total calculated service charge.
